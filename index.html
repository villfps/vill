About Us

Welcome to "Agent's Feast: A Valorant Culinary Experience," where gaming meets gourmet in an unprecedented fusion of excitement and flavor! Our restaurant is the brainchild of passionate gamers and food enthusiasts, united by a vision to create a dining experience that seamlessly blends the strategic world of Valorant with the artistry of gourmet cuisine. Immerse yourself in an atmosphere that captures the essence of Valorant's thrilling universe. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll embark on a culinary journey that mirrors the strategic intensity of the game itself. Our carefully crafted menu draws inspiration from the diverse characters and landscapes of Valorant, offering a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. At Agent's Feast, we pride ourselves on elevating traditional dining to new heights. Our chefs, much like skilled agents, meticulously curate each dish to ensure a perfect balance of textures, aromas, and tastes. Whether you're a dedicated gamer or a food connoisseur, our restaurant invites you to savor the unique blend of entertainment and gastronomy. As you dine amidst themed decor that transports you to the heart of Valorant's action, you'll find that every aspect of Agent's Feast is designed to capture the spirit of the game. Our attentive staff, dressed as your favorite agents, are here to guide you through this immersive experience, ensuring that your time with us is nothing short of extraordinary. Join us at Agent's Feast, where every meal is a celebration of skill, strategy, and exceptional taste. Immerse yourself in the ultimate Valorant-themed restaurant, where the worlds of gaming and gourmet collide to create an unforgettable dining adventure.

Menu

Omen's Cluckmaster

a flavorful delight that combines grilled chicken, seasoned with a unique blend of herbs, on a bed of quinoa infused with mysterious spices. Topped with a bold salsa featuring Phoenix's fire and Sage's healing herbs, it's a taste sensation that takes your taste buds to new dimensions. Omen's Cluckmaster – where the tactical meets the delectable.

Jett's Special Noodles

Experience culinary finesse with Jett's Zen Noodles, inspired by Valorant's agile duelist. Wok-tossed rice noodles showcase Jett's nimble essence, paired with crisp veggies, tofu, and sesame-infused sauce. Each bite is a burst of precision and balance, inviting you to savor the spirit of Jett's dynamic approach.

Viper's Venom Salad

A refreshing mix of crisp greens, grilled chicken, and a tangy citrus kick. Viper's Venom Salad adds a touch of Valorant flair to your plate!

Phoenix Flame Grilled

ndulge in our Phoenix Flame Grilled Steak – succulent premium beef, bold spices, and expert flame-grilling for the perfect char. Served with smoky roasted vegetables, this fiery celebration of robust flavors will leave you craving every juicy bite.

Cypher's Secret Soup

Explore the mystery of flavor with Cypher's Secret Soup. Fresh veggies, tender chicken, and a proprietary herb-spice blend create a symphony of tastes. Like Cypher's surveillance skills, each spoonful reveals a hidden surprise. Savor the secrecy in every bite.

Raze's Boom Burger

An explosive feast for your taste buds! Succulent beef patty grilled to perfection, topped with smoky bacon, melted cheddar, and a zesty chipotle mayo. Encased in a golden brioche bun, Raze's Boom Burger is a flavor explosion that will leave you craving more.

Killjoy's Kickin' Calamari

Spicy calamari rings served with a zesty dipping sauce, representing Killjoy's fiery and tech-savvy personality.

Sova's Arrowhead Risotto

A savory risotto dish with a medley of wild mushrooms, inspired by Sova's connection to nature. Infused with a hint of truffle oil and topped with crispy fried arrowhead-shaped garnishes, this dish embodies the precision and keen instincts of Sova. Delight in the culinary mastery of Sova's Arrowhead Risotto.

Sage's Victory Sundae

A celebratory sundae with various toppings and flavors, representing the victory and teamwork of the Valorant agents.
